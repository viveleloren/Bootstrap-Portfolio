I was born and raised in California. Initially raised in Palo Alto, my parents relocated to Roseville, Ca when I was seven years old. My interests range from chronic reading, and avoiding the outdoors whenever possible. My ideal day is a fall downpour, paired with a wool blanket and a large french press coffee.

While I am perfectly content to not see another face for several days, when I eventually emerge from my hibernation - I will usually haunt my local cafe or the library. I tend to gravitate towards expansive places where staying quiet is considered the standard.
